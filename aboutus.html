ABOUT THE GAME

PUBG MOBILE is a Battle Royale mobile game created independently by Lightspeed & Quantum Studios of Tencent Games, officially licensed by PLAYERUNKNOWNâ€™S BATTLEGROUNDS. It was released globally in March 2018. Built with Unreal Engine 4, PUBG MOBILE focuses on visual quality, maps, shooting experience, and other aspects, providing an all-rounded surreal Battle Royale experience to players. A hundred players will land on the battleground to begin an intense yet fun journey. Each player is required to scavenge survival supplies, as well as to exploit the maximum potential of the map terrain, vehicles, and items to become the last team standing on the battleground. Check your heartbeat and be on guard as the playzone begins to shrink. Multiple maps are available for players to choose from, ranging from Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok, Livik, and many more. Don't forget about the array of gameplay that awaits your exploration! Till today, the influence of PUBG MOBILE does not merely serve its purpose as a game, it is also a hub that connects players from all over the world and a brand that encourages players to live to their fullest.

SECURITY CENTER UPGRADE


Dear Players,

  • Added a new video column - We will provide Investigator live broadcasts, which will help players to better understand an Investigator's role and responsibilities. We will also provide interesting security related videos, to inform players on common security info, and to strengthen our players' anti-cheating awareness.
  • New written info column - We will provide all the latest security related info, Q&As and security news.


New Review System

  • We have designed a new page layout for players to join the Investigators. Players can more easily check the requirements and perks of joining the Investigators. To better maintain the integrity of the game environment, we have added a new oath system. Players must swear an oath in order to join the Investigators.
  • We've newly designed an interface to check Investigator information, Review Records and more. Players can now more easily check their own Review Records.
  • We have also redesigned the Investigator Challenge Missions. At the same time, Investigator Titles and item rewards will automatically convert to the new Investigator Ranks etc.
  • We have added 6 new Titles for Investigators. Get great rewards with faster and more convenient missions. Past Investigators will automatically get the corresponding new Title and rewards.
  • Now an announcement will be sent during the plane flight sequence when any Investigator has joined the match, regardless of Investigator Rank.

New Investigator System

We have redesigned the interface for Investigators, adding great rewards and in-game perks. For more details please see the Version 1.8 Investigator System Update Introduction.

Report Page Improvements

We have added a new way of displaying report reasons. Players can now more easily find the reason through related report reasons.
